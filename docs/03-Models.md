# Models

## Segment aggregate
### Segment
- represents a sequence of candlestics which are part of a defined trend, either uptrend or downtrend
- characterised by a sequence of break of structure (BOS)
- terminated by a change of character (CHoCH)

### Break Of Structure
- when an uptrend breaks a higher high or a downtrend breaks a lower low
- indicates continuation of the current trend

### Change Of Character
- in a downtrend this is when a higher high occurs after a lower low, or in a uptrend a lower low occurs after a higher high
- indicates a changing trend
